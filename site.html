Position Overview
We are seeking an experienced and responsible Site Supervisor to manage and oversee installation teams at various project sites across Maharashtra. The ideal candidate will have strong leadership skills, technical knowledge of mechanical equipment, and the ability to coordinate between teams, clients, and management to ensure successful project completion.
Key Responsibilities
  • Manage and supervise installation teams including Fitters, Helpers, and Technicians
  • Ensure work completion according to technical specifications and project timelines
  • Coordinate with clients for project updates, requirements, and follow-ups
  • Monitor and maintain quality standards throughout the installation process
  • Conduct daily site inspections and safety checks
  • Plan and allocate daily work assignments to team members
  • Manage material inventory and ensure availability of required tools and equipment
  • Resolve on-site technical issues and troubleshoot installation problems
  • Prepare and submit daily progress reports to management
  • Ensure compliance with safety protocols and company policies
  • Train and guide team members on proper installation techniques
  • Handle client queries and maintain positive customer relationships
  • Coordinate with suppliers for timely delivery of materials
Required Skills & Qualifications
  • HSC (12th standard) minimum qualification required
  • 1-3 years of experience in site supervision, installation, or manufacturing
  • Strong understanding of mechanical equipment and installation processes
  • Excellent leadership and team management skills
  • Good communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Ability to read and interpret technical drawings and specifications
  • Problem-solving skills and decision-making capability
  • Knowledge of safety regulations and quality standards
  • Proficiency in Hindi or Marathi; English is a plus
  • Willingness to travel and stay in various cities across Maharashtra
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Customer service orientation
