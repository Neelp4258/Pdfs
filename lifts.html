Ambivare Elevator Management System is a centralized digital platform designed to simplify, automate, and monitor every stage of elevator operations — from inventory to installation, maintenance, and billing.

Key Features

ModuleDescription
DashboardDisplays total parts, warehouse stock, active projects, and maintenance alerts with graphical analytics for real-time visibility.
Inventory ManagementTrack stock across Warehouse, Office, and Service Vans with barcode scanning, PDF reports, and low-stock notifications.
Project ManagementManage elevator installations with client details, timelines, and material allocation to each project site.
Maintenance SchedulerSchedule, assign, and monitor maintenance tasks by priority, technician, and due date to ensure zero downtime.
Billing & InvoicingCreate GST-compliant invoices and quotations with automated tax, discount, and total calculations, all exportable as PDFs.
User ControlRole-based secure login system for Admins and Technicians to ensure controlled and accountable operations.
Activity LogsAutomatic record of every user action for transparency and internal auditing.
Company SettingsCentralized branding for reports, invoices, and communication documents.
