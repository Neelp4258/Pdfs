SEQUENCE OF EVENTS:
  1. On September 29, 2025 at 11:00 AM, our site supervisor noticed a sudden and significant decrease in our material inventory.
  2. Upon investigation and review of CCTV footage, we discovered that Raushan Singh, a labour employed by contractor Dharma Nirmal, was captured on camera stealing our materials on September 22, 2025, between 8:30 PM to 9:00 PM.
  3. Our supervisor was occupied at another site from 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM on September 29, 2025.
  4. At 8:00 PM on September 29, 2025, our supervisor confronted Raushan Singh, who admitted and confessed to stealing the materials.
  5. It was revealed that Raushan Singh had been hiding the stolen materials in his labour camp located within the project premises.
  6. The stolen materials were subsequently sold to Khalid Ahmed, a scrap dealer (Bhangar Wala), whose establishment is located approximately 200 meters from the project site.
  7. This is not an isolated incident. The site supervisor of Dharma Nirmal, Gadadhar, had previously caught another labour stealing our inventory and had issued a warning, but no concrete action was taken at that time.
