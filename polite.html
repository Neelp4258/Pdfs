Respected Sir/Madam,
We, Maxim Parking Solution, would like to respectfully bring to your kind attention a serious matter regarding theft and misappropriation of our inventory materials at the Avishkar Commercial Infrastructure Project site in Kharadi, Pune, where we have been entrusted with the Automated Parking System Installation.
BACKGROUND:
Our company has been working diligently on the aforementioned project with full dedication. However, over the past few months, we have been experiencing theft of materials from our inventory stored at the project site. Despite our previous informal communications regarding this matter, we unfortunately continue to face these challenges, resulting in significant financial impact on our operations.
DETAILS OF THE INCIDENT:
Contractor Name:
Dharma Nirmal (Civil Services Contractor)

Raushan Singh (Labour of Dharma Nirmal)

September 22, 2025 (Between 8:30 PM to 9:00 PM)
Date of Discovery:
September 29, 2025 (11:00 AM)
Date of Discussion:
September 29, 2025 (8:00 PM)
Our Site Supervisor:
Akhilesh Singh
Storage Location:
Labour camp at the project site premises
Material Recipient:
Khalid Ahmed (Scrap Dealer) - Located approximately 200 meters from the site
SEQUENCE OF EVENTS:
  1. On September 29, 2025 at 11:00 AM, our site supervisor Mr. Akhilesh Singh noticed a significant decrease in our material inventory.
  2. Upon investigation and review of available footage, we discovered that Raushan Singh, a worker employed by contractor Dharma Nirmal, was involved in taking our materials on September 22, 2025, between 8:30 PM to 9:00 PM.
  3. Our supervisor was attending to responsibilities at another site from 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM on September 29, 2025.
  4. At 8:00 PM on September 29, 2025, our supervisor had a discussion with Raushan Singh, who acknowledged taking the materials.
  5. It was revealed that the materials had been stored in the labour camp located within the project premises.
  6. The materials were subsequently transferred to Khalid Ahmed, a scrap dealer, whose establishment is located approximately 200 meters from the project site.
  7. We understand that similar incidents have occurred previously, and the site supervisor had earlier addressed another worker regarding material handling, though no formal action was taken at that time.
