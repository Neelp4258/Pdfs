Position Overview
We are looking for hardworking and reliable Helpers to support our installation teams. The Helper will provide essential assistance during the installation of car parking systems at various sites across Maharashtra. This is an excellent entry-level opportunity for individuals eager to learn and grow in the manufacturing and installation industry.
Key Responsibilities
  • Provide assistance and support to the installation team, including Fitters and Technicians
  • Help in material handling, loading, and unloading of equipment and tools
  • Prepare and organize tools and materials required for installation activities
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the worksite
  • Support fitters during the assembly and installation of parking systems
  • Carry materials and equipment to required locations at the site
  • Assist in basic measurements and marking as directed by supervisors
  • Follow safety guidelines and maintain a safe work environment
  • Perform general maintenance and cleaning of tools and equipment
  • Report any safety hazards or issues to the site supervisor
Required Skills & Qualifications
  • Any educational qualification accepted
  • 0-2 years of experience in helper, labor, or similar roles (fresher candidates welcome)
  • Physically fit and able to perform manual labor
  • Ability to lift and carry heavy materials
  • Willingness to learn and follow instructions
  • Good team player with positive attitude
  • Willingness to travel and stay in various cities across Maharashtra
  • Basic communication skills in Hindi or Marathi
  • Punctual and reliable work ethic
What We Offer
  • Competitive salary package of ₹10,000 - ₹15,000 per month
  • All traveling expenses covered by the company
  • Accommodation and food expenses fully covered
  • On-the-job training and skill development opportunities
  • Career growth potential to advance to Fitter or Technician roles
  • Supportive team environment
  • Exposure to innovative parking system technologies
