Position Overview
We are looking for responsible and customer-oriented Parking Operators to manage and operate automated parking systems at residential and commercial buildings. The Parking Operator will be the primary point of contact for residents and customers, ensuring smooth and efficient parking operations while maintaining high standards of service.
Key Responsibilities
  • Operate parking systems according to residents' and customers' requirements
  • Ensure smooth functioning of automated parking solutions throughout the day
  • Assist customers with vehicle parking and retrieval processes
  • Maintain accurate system logs and records of all parking transactions
  • Monitor parking system performance and report any technical issues immediately
  • Provide excellent customer service to residents and building visitors
  • Perform routine checks and basic maintenance of parking equipment
  • Manage parking space allocation efficiently
  • Handle customer queries and resolve minor operational issues
  • Ensure cleanliness and organization of the parking facility
  • Follow all safety protocols and emergency procedures
  • Coordinate with maintenance teams for repairs and servicing
  • Work in shift patterns including evenings, weekends, and holidays as required
Required Skills & Qualifications
  • Any educational qualification accepted
  • Valid Driving License is mandatory
  • 0-2 years of experience in operations, customer service, or related field
  • Good understanding of vehicle handling and parking procedures
  • Excellent customer service and communication skills
  • Ability to work in shift patterns (day/night/weekend shifts)
  • Basic technical aptitude to operate automated systems
  • Problem-solving abilities and quick decision-making skills
  • Professional appearance and courteous behavior
  • Proficiency in Hindi or Marathi; English is a plus
  • Physical fitness to stand for extended periods
  • Punctual and reliable with good attendance record
