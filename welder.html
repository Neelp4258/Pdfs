Position Overview
We are seeking skilled and experienced Welders to join our manufacturing and installation team. The role involves fabrication and welding of car parking system structures and components at our manufacturing facility and various installation sites across Maharashtra. The ideal candidate should have expertise in metal welding techniques, strong attention to quality, and the ability to work on both manufacturing and on-site projects.
Key Responsibilities
  • Perform arc welding, MIG/TIG welding, and gas welding on steel structures for car parking systems
  • Fabricate and weld metal frames, support structures, and parking equipment components
  • Read and interpret technical drawings, blueprints, and welding specifications
  • Conduct on-site welding work during installation of car parking systems at various locations
  • Ensure all welding work meets quality standards and structural integrity requirements
  • Inspect welded joints and structures for defects, cracks, or weak points
  • Cut, shape, and join metal parts using appropriate welding techniques
  • Maintain welding equipment, tools, and machinery in proper working condition
  • Follow safety protocols including use of protective gear and safe welding practices
  • Coordinate with fabrication team and site supervisors for project completion
  • Perform grinding, cleaning, and finishing of welded surfaces
  • Work on modifications and repairs of parking system structures as needed
Required Skills & Qualifications
  • Any educational qualification accepted (ITI in Welding preferred)
  • 6 months to 3 years of proven experience in welding work, preferably in manufacturing or installation
  • Proficiency in arc welding, MIG/TIG welding, and gas welding techniques
  • Ability to read and understand technical drawings and welding symbols
  • Strong knowledge of metal properties and welding standards
  • Experience working with steel and metal fabrication
  • Good hand-eye coordination and steady hands for precision welding
  • Physical fitness and ability to work in various positions (standing, bending, overhead)
  • Understanding of safety protocols and commitment to workplace safety
  • Willingness to travel and work at different sites across Maharashtra
  • Basic communication skills in Hindi or Marathi
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
